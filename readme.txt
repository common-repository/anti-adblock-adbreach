== Description ==

= General description =
Do you even have a decrease in your advertisement revenue according to users of AdBlock? In this case, you should use Adbreach. Adbreach offers the opportunity to block Adblockers. Thereby, you can display your website´s advertisement to your clients. 

= Importance of Advertisement =
21 percent of the advertisement in Germany is stopped by adblockers. Mobile adblocker on IOS and Android are getting more and more popular. The advertising partners enter into less partnerships with websites whose advertisements are blocked. 

= Global Facts and Trends =
In 2015 the use of adblocker increased by 42 percent. There was an economic loss of 22 billion USD. This trend is going to continue. Currently 180 million people use adblockers. 

= Benefit =
We support you in using your hidden potential, by displaying blocked advertisements. The plugin can be downloaded for free. 

= Functions =
Create non-blockable advertisement pictures: Adbreach saves your advertisement dynamically with changing names for adblock users. This procedure ensures that adblocker can be blocked. 

Display Popups: Don’t let your popups be blocked by adblocker. With the registration you can manage your popups. 

= Security =
We don’t change your advertisement or any kind of data. 

== Installation ==


1. Load the plugin into the list /wp-content/plugins/adbreach/ or install it via the Installation-button of Wordpress.
1. Activate the plugin via the overview of plugins.
1. Insert your personal verification code. You receive it through the [registration](https://adbreach.de/register).
